Legislative History

1. Adopted by the Tribal Council in June 1, 2004, Resolution Number 026-04. Adoption required amendments to Chapter Chapter TCT.05 and Chapter GMG.02 by same resolution. Approved by BIA June 15, 2004.

2. Sections Section YAE.09.01, Section YAE.09.03(a), Section YAE.09.06 are amended, sections 28.2(H), Section YAE.09.05(a), Section YAE.09.05(c) are deleted; sections Section YAE.09.03(c) and Section YAE.09.12 are created; Section YAE.09.05 is renumbered on October 5, 2004 by Tribal Council Resolution No. 052-04 to add a filing fee, clarify how the court is to notify the Treasurer, clarify that delivery to the Treasurer occurs 45 days prior to a per capita payment, add in future amendment section, and delete references to wage assignments because 28 U.S.C. §1738B requires full faith and credit of foreign child support orders. Approved by BIA on November 8, 2004.

3. Sections Section YAE.09.02(a); Section YAE.09.03(c), (E); Section YAE.09.04(c); Section YAE.09.12 are created; sections 28.3(A)(1), (B); Section YAE.09.06, Section YAE.09.06(a), (A)(1), (B), (B)(1), (B)(2) are amended; sections Section YAE.09.02, Section YAE.09.13, Section YAE.09.01 are renumbered; and sections Section YAE.09.03(b)(2) and Section YAE.09.04(c) are deleted by Tribal Council on September 20, 2005, Resolution No. 046-05.

4. On November 6th, 2012, the Tribal Council repealed existing Chapter YAE.09 and adopted a new Chapter YAE.09 that added new sections, renumbered, re-organized and revised existing sections in order to establish a tribal child support agency in accordance with federal law. This was done by Resolution Number 009-13.

5. In order to meet requirements as identified by the Office of Child Support Enforcement, Sections 28.3 (P), Section YAE.09.07(c) are amended; Section YAE.09.12 moved to its own section; 28.2 (B) is deleted; and beginning at Section YAE.09.03 the chapter is renumbered throughout by Tribal Council on May 29, 2013, Resolution No. 054-13.

6. On November 18th, 2014, Tribal Council repealed the existing Chapter YAE.09 Child Support Order and adopted a new Chapter YAE.09 Child Support to accommodate whole-scale changes, including adding new sections and re-numbering, re-organizing and revising existing sections, and re-naming it as the "Child Support Ordinance". This was done by Resolution No. 08-15.

7. Sections Section YAE.09.01.2; Section YAE.09.01.5; Section YAE.09.04; Section YAE.09.06(c) & (D); Section YAE.09.13(e) & (G); Section YAE.09.14(a)(4) & (B); and Section YAE.09.07(a) are clarified and amended; and sections 28.32 (D) & (E); Section YAE.09.04; Section YAE.09.05 par. 1 & Section YAE.09.05(c); Section YAE.09.07(b), (C) and (D) are added; and a portion of Section YAE.09.05(a) has been deleted by Tribal Council on September 19, 2017, Resolution No. 050-17.
