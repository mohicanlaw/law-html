Legislative History

1. Legislative Note: Chapter 20 initially part of Conservation Code, which was adopted by the Tribal Council on November 14, 1978; for additional Legislative History on Conservation Code, see Chapter ENV.02.

2. Amended by Council motion of Council, July 15, 1997. New provisions include allowing minor-aged direct descendants to hunt during tribal season, shortening of deer gun season; adding turkey season guidelines; changing brook trout and bass bag limits; prohibiting 4-wheel drive vehicles on logging roads during spring break up; closing road south of Richard's Bridge; mandatory registration; wildlife count.

3. Amended on March 21, 2000 by Resolution No. 010-2000. Approval by BIA April 11, 2000.

4. Section ENV.01.09(a) amended creating deer donation policy by Tribal Council on January 2, 2002, Resolution Number 03-02. Approved by BIA January 15, 2002.

5. Legislative Note: The March 21, 2000 amendments regarding fees were not properly incorporated into the Ordinance. Upon this discovery, the appropriate fees were incorporated into the Ordinance on November 5, 2003, following the November 4, 2003 Tribal Council meeting.

6. On July 20, 2004, by Resolution No. 039-04, Tribal Council amended sections Section ENV.01.01(a) and (B), Section ENV.01.11(c), Section ENV.01.13 by inserting "and the Stockbridge-Munsee Fish and Game Board;" sections Section ENV.01.02(a) and (B) by deleting requirement to obtain State of Wisconsin licenses; sections Section ENV.01.03(a) and (B) by changing the fees; creating Section ENV.01.03(c); deleting section 21.6(5) "Fur bearing animal"; deleting "bald eagle, elk, fisher, golden eagle, marten, timber wolf, lynx or any species determined to be rare or" from section 21.6(C)(4); adding "no more than ten grouse" to Section ENV.01.07(d); adding sections Section ENV.01.08(e) and 21.10(E); creating reporting requirement to section 21.9(A)(4); amending sections 21.9(A)(2) and Section ENV.01.09(b) to clarify the type and size of weapon or firearm; deleted "the restrictions on the manner for taking bear are the same as outlined in (A)(1) of this section" from Section ENV.01.09(b)(1); creating Section ENV.01.11(b)(1); and creating Section ENV.01.14.

7. Amended by Tribal Council on June 6, 2006, by creating Section ENV.01.09(d), Resolution No. 044-06.

8. Amended by Tribal Council on July 18, 2006, by creating sections 21.2(D), 21.3(A)(4), 21.3(B)(3), Section ENV.01.08(a), 21.8(B)(1)(2)(3), 21.8 (C)(1)(2), and 21.11(A)(1)(2)(3); and amending section 21.3(A)(3), Resolution No. 051-06.

9. Amended by Tribal Council on November 7, 2006, by deleting, "Tribal members may request their harvest tags be mailed to them," from Section 21.11(A)(1), Resolution No. 011-07.

10. On May 6, 2008, Tribal Council repealed existing Chapter ENV.01 and adopted a new Chapter ENV.01 that renumbered and had changes clarifying numerous sections and incorporated enforcement and other relevant sections of Chapter 20. This was done by Resolution No. 030-08.

11. On October 9, 2008, Tribal Council made an administrative correction by adding the words "or permittee" to Section ENV.01.15.5 (C).

12. Amendments adopted by Tribal Council on January 6, 2009 to create new Sections Section ENV.01.03(p), Section ENV.01.07(f), 21.10 (D), Section ENV.01.13(c)(1), 21.13 (E), 21.15 (B)(4), and 21.20 (EE); renumber Section 21.10 (D) as 21.10 (E); amend Section ENV.01.16(a); move Section 21.15 (A)(2) to be Section 21.15 (F); and make a technical change to Section ENV.01.21. On February 3, 2009, Tribal Council renumbered the resolution adopting amendments as Resolution No. 021-09.

13. Amendment adopted by Tribal Council on September 1, 2009 to amend Sections Section ENV.01.13, 21.16 (A)(3) and 21.20(D); and to create new Sections Section ENV.01.13(c)(2) and Section ENV.01.13(c)(3). This was done by Resolution No. 082-09.

14. On November 16, 2010, the Tribal Council adopted changes to Chapter ENV.01 amending Sections Section ENV.01.08(c), Section ENV.01.15.5(C)(1), and Section ENV.01.15.5(G) and creating new Sections Section ENV.01.03(q), Section ENV.01.08(a)(1), Section ENV.01.08(g) and 21.20(FF). These changes were adopted by Resolution No. 06-11.

15. On June 5, 2012, the Tribal Council adopted the following changes to Chapter ENV.01:

  • Amending Section ENV.01.02(a), Section ENV.01.04, 21.15(B)(2), Section ENV.01.15.5(A), Section ENV.01.15.5(B), Section ENV.01.15.5(C), Section ENV.01.17(b), Section ENV.01.19(a), 21.20(V), Section ENV.01.21(a) and Section ENV.01.21(b); and
  • Deleting Section ENV.01.08(a)(1), Section ENV.01.08(c)(1), Section ENV.01.15(d), 21.15(E), Section ENV.01.15.5(D)-(G), 21.17(F)(2)(d), 21.17(G)(1) and 21.21(B)(3)(a)-(b); and
  • Creating new Section ENV.01.02(b), Section ENV.01.12(c), Section ENV.01.12(d), 21.15(A)(1)(c), Section ENV.01.17(a)(1)-(3), 21.20(GG) and 21.21(B)(2)(a)-(b); and
  • Renumbering 21.17(F)(2)(e)-(g), 21.17(G)(2)-(4), Section ENV.01.19 and Section ENV.01.21; and
  • Technical change Section ENV.01.06(b), Section ENV.01.08(h), Section ENV.01.17(c) and 21.21(D)(1).

These changes were adopted by Resolution No. 040-12.

16. On October 23, 2012, the Tribal Council adopted Resolution No. 007-13, which authorized changes to Chapter ENV.01 to add requirement that individuals be at least 12-years of age, clarifying the time frame for permits and seasons, and to clarify other language. These changes are: amending Section ENV.01.08(a) to create subsection (1) and a new subsection (2); amending Section ENV.01.08(b); amending Section ENV.01.08(c) to create a new subsection (1) and subsection (2); amending Section ENV.01.08(h); amending 21.9 (A)(2) and (3); amending Section ENV.01.09(b)(1), (2) and (3); amending Section ENV.01.16(a)(1); and amending 21.21 (B)(2)(a).

17. On November 5, 2013, the Tribal Council adopted Resolution No. 07-14, which authorized changes to Chapter ENV.01 adding a new deer sustenance tag, allowing mentor hunting by 10- and 11-year olds, and clarifying required permits and fees for permittees who are minors. These changes are: amending subSection ENV.01.08(a)(2); amending subSection ENV.01.08(c)(1); amending subSection ENV.01.08(d); adding new subSection ENV.01.08(i); adding new subparts 21.8 (I)(1)-(4); adding new subsection 21.8 (J); adding new subparts 21.8 (J)(1)-(4) and amending subSection ENV.01.09(b)(2).

18. On August 19, 2014, the Tribal Council adopted Resolution No. 043-14, which authorized changes to Chapter ENV.01 to limit baiting and feeding to control the spread of chronic wasting disease, as well as to address using resources responsibly, tagging of equipment, hunting by permittees, turkey harvest and hunting bear with dogs. These changes are to add sections Section ENV.01.12.5, Section ENV.01.01(i), 21.8(K), 21.8(L), Section ENV.01.15(c)(3), and Section ENV.01.15(d); and to amend sections 21.9(A)(4), Section ENV.01.14(b)(1), Section ENV.01.14(c)(1)-(3), Section ENV.01.15(c), and 21.20(E).

19. On June 3, 2015, the Tribal Council adopted Resolution No. 048-15, which authorized clarifications in relation to turkey hunting fees for permittees, the trout season for tribal members and citations. These changes amend subsections 21.9(A)(4), Section ENV.01.09(b)(3), 21.10(D), and 21.21 (B)(1)(b), (C), (C)(4), (D)(2) and (D)(3).

20. On September 6, 2016, the Tribal Council adopted Resolution No. 063-16, which authorized changes to Chapter ENV.01 to rename a fishing pond, clarify use of lead shot, and modify hunting seasons. The changes amend subsections 21.10(E), Section ENV.01.12.5(C), Section ENV.01.13(c)(1), Section ENV.01.14(b)(2) and Section ENV.01.15(a) and create new subsections 21.13(E) (1) and (2).

21. On August 15, 2017, the Tribal Council adopted Resolution No. 042-17, which authorized changes to Chapter ENV.01 to: redefine big game to not include turkey; define adult bear and cub; retitle department names; increase age for permittee fishing to 16; eliminate fees for minor-age direct descendants to hunt and fish; and eliminate the buck-only season. These changes:

  • Amend sections: Section ENV.01.03(a),(C) and (J); Section ENV.01.05(b)(5); Section ENV.01.06(a),(C) and (D); Section ENV.01.07(a); Section ENV.01.08(d) and (H); Section ENV.01.09(b); Section ENV.01.14(a); Section ENV.01.15(a) and(C); Section ENV.01.16(a), (B) and (C); Section ENV.01.17(a)(3), (F)(7) and (G); Section ENV.01.18(a); and Section ENV.01.20(a), (Q).
  • Create the following new sections: Section ENV.01.03(r) and (S); and Section ENV.01.15(c)(2).
  • Delete the following sections: 21.9(B)(1-3); and 21.15 (B)(4).

22. Resolution No. 069-19, adopted September 3, 2019.
