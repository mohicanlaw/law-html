Resolution No. 012-12 — Gaming

Legislative History

The amendments to Chapter GMG.01 are as follows:

  • Changes or additions to Sections: Preamble; 4.1; 4.2; 4.5; Section TCT.04.02; 4.18; Section YAE.02.03.3; 9.12.1; 9.12.2; 9.12.30; 9.12.Section BUS.02.01.4; 9.14; 9.15; 9.15.1; Section YAE.04.01; Section YAE.04.04.4; Section YAE.04.04.6; Section YAE.04.04.8; Section YAE.04.04.9; 10.10; Section YAE.05.02; Section YAE.05.05; Section YAE.05.06.1; Section YAE.05.06.3; Section YAE.05.06.4; Section YAE.08.02; Section YAE.08.04.1; Section YAE.08.05; Section YAE.08.10.2; Section YAE.08.10.3; 15.15; 15.18.3; 15.18.4; Section SAF.02.08; Section TCT.05.01; Section ENV.01.02.2; Section ENV.01.02.3; Section ENV.01.02.4; Section ENV.01.02.5; Section ENV.01.02.10; Section ENV.01.02.19; Section ENV.01.02.21; and Section ENV.01.02.26;
  • Deletion of Sections: 14.14.2 and 14.14.6 (with 14.14 then renumbered accordingly); and Section SAF.02.09;
  • Addition of new Sections: 4.18.1 .1-.12; 4.18.2; 4.18.3; 4.18.4; Section TCT.04.06.4; Section YAE.04.04.4.1; Section YAE.04.04.4.2; Section YAE.04.04.10; 10.11; Section YAE.05.03.5; Section YAE.08.10.8; Section YAE.08.10.9; 15.16.2 and 15.16.3 (with 15.16 then renumbered accordingly); and 15.18.6 (with 15.18.7 then renumbered); and
  • Minor language clarifications in Sections: 4.2.3; 4.2.5; Section YAE.01.01; Section YAE.03.02; Section YAE.03.03; 9.12.8; 9.12.13; Section YAE.08.03.2.3; Section YAE.08.03.2.10; Section YAE.08.04.3; Section YAE.08.04.4; Section YAE.08.06.7; Section YAE.08.11.2; Section YAE.08.12.1; Section YAE.08.12.4; 15.18.2; 15.18.5; Section SAF.02.01.2; and Section SAF.02.05.
Amendments submitted to NIGC for approval. Tribe informed by NIGC that corrections needed to Sections Section GMG.01.15.3.4 and Section GMG.01.15.3.7 so that language matches federal regulations. Corrected amendments, which included changes to Sections Section GMG.01.15.3.4 and Section GMG.01.15.3.7, re-adopted by the Tribal Council on January 30, 2012, by Resolution No. 017-12. Corrected amendments submitted to NIGC for approval and approved.
